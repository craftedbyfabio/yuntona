# Zenity

Governance and security for Low-Code/No-Code AI agents and copilots.

## What It Does

A governance and security platform for Low-Code/No-Code AI agents and copilots. Provides visibility, risk assessment, and policy enforcement for AI-powered automations built by business users rather than developers.
